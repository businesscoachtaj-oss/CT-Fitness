Section 05 ~10 min

Movement
Screen

Basic movement quality check. Score 1–3 for each pattern. Use the notes field for anything specific. Don't overthink it — this is a starting point, not a clinical assessment.

1 — Compensated / painful / can't perform
2 — Performed with noticeable compensation
3 — Clean, controlled movement
Bodyweight Squat
Feet shoulder-width, toes slightly out. Arms forward or overhead. Squat to depth — watch knees, torso lean, heel lift, and depth.
Hip Hinge (Romanian Deadlift)
Feet hip-width. Push hips back, maintain neutral spine. Watch lower back rounding, hamstring tightness, and weight shift.
Push-Up (or Wall Push)
Full or knee push-up. Watch scapula control, elbow flare, lower back sag, and chin poke. Modify to wall if needed.
Shoulder / Pull Pattern
Arms overhead reach. Can they get arms overhead without lower back arching? Watch shoulder mobility, thoracic extension, and lat tightness.
Single Leg Balance
Stand on one leg for 5 seconds, eyes open. Repeat both sides. Watch hip drop, ankle wobble, and upper body sway.
Reverse Lunge
Step one foot back, lower to knee. Watch front knee tracking, torso lean, balance, and hip mobility. Both sides.
