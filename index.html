Who This Is For

Doesn't matter
where you're at.

Good coaching isn't reserved for athletes or people with perfect schedules. It's for anyone ready to be consistent — whether that's with their training, their nutrition, or just building a routine that sticks. If you're willing to show up, I'll make sure it's worth it.

🏁

Just Getting Started

You've decided to take training seriously but don't know where to begin. We'll build a solid foundation, teach you the movements properly, and make sure your first few months actually set you up for long-term progress.

📈

Stuck at a Plateau

You've been training for a while but progress has stalled. Could be programming, recovery, nutrition — usually a mix. Fresh eyes on your training often make all the difference.

⏱️

Time-Poor but Motivated

Life is busy. You don't have time to waste on training that doesn't work. Your program will be built around your actual schedule — not an ideal one that doesn't exist.

🎯

Training Towards Something

A goal — aesthetic, performance, or somewhere in between. Having a specific target makes programming more purposeful and keeps you moving in one clear direction.

🤝

Needs Accountability

You know what you should be doing — you just don't always do it alone. Having someone who checks in, notices, and adjusts changes everything. Most people underestimate how much this matters.

💡

Wants to Actually Learn

Not just told what to do, but understanding why. The goal is for you to eventually know your body well enough that you could program for yourself — a good coach works towards that, not against it.

A Bit About Me

Hey, I'm Taj.
Let's get to work.

I started CT Personal Training because I genuinely believe movement is medicine — and that everyone deserves coaching that treats them like an individual, not a template. Whether it's getting stronger, losing body fat, or just building habits that actually stick, the approach is always the same: honest, practical, and built around your real life.

Nutrition is just as much a part of this as training. I've seen people put in serious effort in the gym and barely move the needle because their diet wasn't supporting it. We look at both — not with rigid meal plans you'll hate, but with education and habits that make sense long-term.

I coach in-person and online, and it's the same price either way. If you can get in for your weekly session, brilliant — I'll be there. If life gets in the way, we catch up on a call. No fuss.

The Process

How it
works.

Simple by design. No long onboarding, no complicated systems — just coaching that starts working from week one.

01

Get in Touch

Fill in the contact form or send a message. Tell me a bit about where you're at, what you're after, and whether you'd prefer in-person or online. No commitment required.

02

Intro Conversation

We'll have a quick chat — in person or on a call — to talk through your goals, training history, schedule, and what a realistic plan looks like. This is where we figure out if we're a good fit.

03

Your Program's Ready

I'll write your first week of programming based on everything we discussed. It lands in your client dashboard before your first session so you know exactly what to expect going in.

04

We Get to Work

Weekly sessions or calls, ongoing support between them, and a program that evolves as you do. This is where it actually happens — and it tends to compound faster than people expect.

What Makes It Work

Training and
nutrition. Both.

Most coaches focus on one or the other. Training without dialling in your nutrition is leaving results on the table — and the same goes the other way around. We work on both, because that's what actually moves the needle.

01

Individualised Programming

Written for you specifically. Adjusted each week based on how you're actually responding — not a fixed schedule you follow blindly.

02

Nutrition That Makes Sense

High protein targets, balanced plates, habits that fit your lifestyle. Education-first — so you understand what you're doing and why, not just following rules.

03

Flexible Check-Ins

In-person or a call — your choice each week. Life changes week to week, and your check-in format can keep up with that.

04

Consistency Over Perfection
