A Bit About Me

Hey, I'm Taj.
Let's get to work.

I started CT Personal Training because I genuinely believe movement is medicine — and that everyone deserves coaching that treats them like an individual, not a template. Whether it's getting stronger, losing body fat, or just building habits that actually stick, the approach is always the same: honest, practical, and built around your real life.

Nutrition is just as much a part of this as training. I've seen people put in serious effort in the gym and barely move the needle because their diet wasn't supporting it. We look at both — not with rigid meal plans you'll hate, but with education and habits that make sense long-term.

I coach in-person and online, and it's the same price either way. If you can get in for your weekly session, brilliant — I'll be there. If life gets in the way, we catch up on a call. No fuss.

The Process

How it
works.

Simple by design. No complicated onboarding — just coaching that starts from week one.

01

Fill in the contact form or send a message. Tell me where you're at, what you're after, and whether you'd prefer in-person or online. No commitment required.

02

Intro Conversation

We'll have a quick chat to talk through your goals, training history, schedule, and what a realistic plan looks like. This is where we figure out if we're a good fit.

Weekly sessions, ongoing support, and a program that evolves as you do. It tends to compound faster than people expect.

What Makes It Work

Training and
nutrition. Both.

Most coaches focus on one or the other. Training without dialling in your nutrition is leaving results on the table. We work on both, because that's what actually moves the needle.

01

Individualised Programming

Written for you specifically. Adjusted each week based on how you're actually responding — not a fixed schedule you follow blindly.

02

Nutrition That Makes Sense

High protein targets, balanced plates, habits that fit your lifestyle. Education-first — so you understand what you're doing and why, not just following rules.

03

Flexible Check-Ins

In-person or a call — your choice each week. Life changes week to week, and your check-in format can keep up with that.
